About Us



Epic 7 Media Communications was envisaged in 2005 when four innovative black entrepreneurs subscribed to a vision of a truly black empowered marketing and communications agency.
Today, the dreams of Obrey Khupe (marketing director), Sivuyile Gulwa (operations director), Nolu- siso Kelembe (financial director) and Leigh Joseph (projects director) have been realised through the launch of Epic 7 Media Communications cc.
The four directors bring with them extensive experience and expertise across the areas of marketing, communications, print & digital media; and publishing. The company has in place a hi-tech infrastruc- ture which enables it to deliver on its pay-off line: You dream it - we'll do it.
